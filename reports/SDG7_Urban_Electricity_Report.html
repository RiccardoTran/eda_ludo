Key Findings

Main Observations

  • Urban electricity access in SSA averages around 80%
  • Access has been steadily improving over the past three decades
  • Several countries have achieved near-universal urban electrification
  • Some countries still have less than 50% urban electricity access
  • The gap between best and worst performing countries is significant

Data Notes

  • Data shows percentage access only, not quality of electricity service
  • Some countries have missing data for certain years
  • National urban averages may hide differences within cities
